Biography
Marija Spasovska born on 5 October 2005 in Skopje,Macedonia is Macedonian singer and actress.


She sang her first song at only 4 years old "My doll" at the festival "Potocinja". From then she has performed more than 10 own songs at diffrent festivals and competitions and has won over twenty awards, comendations and diplomas from different music festivals and competitions at the domestic and international level.

In 2017 she won a competition "Casting Talent Show" in Macedonia and got a role in the first Macedonian musical "My Secret" dedicated to Tose Proeski.

Her first teenage song "Play with heart" was released on May 30, 2018.
